About Me

As a game developer, I have a passion for creating immersive and engaging experiences using Unreal Engine's Blueprint and C++. I have completed a degree in game design at Quasar Institute for Advanced Design, where I produced many different games using Unity and Unreal Engine. Additionally, I have basic knowledge of Maya, Blender, Zbrush, Wwise, Ableton, and Adobe suite programs. I have recently completed a certificate in Unreal Engine Gameplay Developer at Rainbow Academy - Digital Entertainment Academy per Animazione e Vfx, where I learned advanced techniques and best practices for developing games with Unreal Engine. I am also proficient in English, having obtained a Cambridge English Level 1 Certificate in ESOL International (First). I value teamwork and collaboration, and I am always eager to learn new skills and technologies. My goal is to become a professional game developer and designer, and to create games that inspire and entertain people.
