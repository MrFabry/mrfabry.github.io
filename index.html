About Me

As a passionate game developer, I bring creativity and technical expertise to every project. With a degree in game design from Quasar Institute and an Unreal Engine Gameplay Developer certificate from Rainbow Academy, I specialize in creating immersive experiences using Unreal Engine's Blueprint and C++. My skills extend to Unity and C# for mobile app and game development, allowing me to craft engaging content across various platforms. I'm also versed in 3D modeling tools and audio software, enhancing my ability to contribute to diverse aspects of game creation. My goal is to push the boundaries of interactive entertainment, inspiring and entertaining people through innovative game design and development.
